    ♥ Red Team Ethical Hacker ♥

    Red teams are “ethical hackers” who help test an organization's defenses by identifying vulnerabilities and launching attacks in a controlled environment. Red teams are opposed by defenders called blue teams, and both parties work together to provide a comprehensive picture of organizational security readiness.

♥ Choice 2: ♥

♥ Computer Forensics Analyst ♥

Computer forensics analysts assist in the investigation of crimes and cybersecurity incidents. Often, they recover hidden, encrypted, and deleted information for law enforcement agencies. They also follow chains of custody to safeguard the integrity of data and ensure its admissibility in court.

♥ Choice 3: ♥

♥ System Administrator ♥

Sysadmins are responsible for managing, troubleshooting, licensing, and updating hardware and software assets. You will ensure that appropriate measures are proactively followed in response to unforeseen issues such as IT downtime or zero-day exploits.
